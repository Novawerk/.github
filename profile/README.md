# Novawerk

> An open, non-profit community hub. We believe innovation shouldn't chase trends — it should create real value for the world.

Anyone can bring an idea, find collaborators, and turn it into a real project. We don't make demos. We make things that have to be handed over.

→ **[novawerk.io](https://novawerk.io)** · est. 2025

---

## What we believe

- **Purposeful Innovation** — when this thing exists, is the world a little better?
- **Build, Learn, Together** — skills are a side-effect of the project. So are friendships.
- **Responsibility for Change** — indifference is the only failure.

## How we work

`01 Validate` → `02 Break Down` → `03 Collaborate` → `04 Build for Real`

Sharpen a vague idea into a clear problem worth solving. Slice ambition into milestones small enough to ship next week. Find the people who complete your team. Hand the thing over.

## Three ways to join

1. **Bring an idea.** Tell us what you want to make. We'll help you check it, find people, and push it to the next step.
2. **Join a project.** Pick one from the [portfolio](https://novawerk.io/portfolio), talk to the lead directly. Lightweight contribution welcome.
3. **Just look around.** [Subscribe to the newsletter](https://novawerk.io), or come to a monthly open day.
